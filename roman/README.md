### <ins>Roman numerals</ins>

Convert arabic number to roman and vice versa.  
Examples:

  Arabic | Roman
 --------|--------
    1    | "I"
    2    | "II"
    3    | "III"
    4    | "IV"
    5    | "V"
    6    | "VI"
    9    | "IX"
    27   | "XXVII"
    48   | "XLVIII"
    59   | "LIX"
    93   | "XCIII"
    141  | "CXLI"
    163  | "CLXIII"
    402  | "CDII"
    575  | "DLXXV"
    911  | "CMXI"
    1024 | "MXXIV"
    3000 | "MMM"

- [Haskell solution](Roman.hs) usage example:
```haskell
*Main> roman 89
"LXXXIX"
*Main> arabic "LXXXIX"
89
```
- [C++ solution](roman.cpp) usage example:
```c++
Enter number: 89
Roman: LXXXIX
Arabic: 89
```
